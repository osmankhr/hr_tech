01

North Star & what we optimize for

One metric tells us which features are worth building. A feature that adds candidates but lowers accept-rate is negative value — the goal is a trusted shortlist, fast, not a bigger list.

Primary metric
Time to a hiring-manager-accepted shortlist

Speed and quality together. We measure from req-open to the moment a hiring manager says "yes, interview these" — not raw candidates returned.

Supporting signals

  • Recruiter adoption & repeat use
  • Shortlist → hiring-manager accept rate
  • Source → interview conversion
  • Candidate response rate on outreach

05

The three bets to champion

If we only fund three things beyond core hardening, these. Each earns its place for a different reason.

BET 02

Relevance feedback loop

Sourcing is "I know it when I see it." A calibration mechanism — show five, recruiter rates, agent tunes within the session and remembers the pattern across sessions — is what makes the tool sticky.

Why it wins: adoption & repeat use, the metric a one-shot search can't move.
BET 06

Internal mobility & silver-medalists

Surface internal staff and strong past applicants before sourcing externally. The non-obvious bet most teams skip because external search is the shiny part.

Why it wins: high ROI, low GDPR risk — lawful basis already in place, data quality far above scraped profiles.
BET 07

Talent-market comparison

Turn the Ankara/İstanbul study into a repeatable location-strategy feature: pool sizing, employer mapping, "where should we hire."

Why it wins: cross-track leverage — the natural bridge into the reporting-insights track of Future of HR.
06

Guardrails — baked in, not bolted on

We're building inside a regulated EU employer. These shape the build from day one rather than getting retrofitted.

Lawful basis & retention

Defined basis and retention on external candidate data. Aggregate market-insight kept separate from named-PII flows.

Fairness & bias

Balanced-slate nudges and audit logging — designed with DPO and works-council input early, given EU hiring sensitivities.

Candidate experience

No spammy automated first impressions. Outreach stays human-reviewed to protect the employer brand.

Human in the loop

Compliance gate before any PII enrichment or send. The recruiter, not the agent, makes the call.

How we'll know it's working
Time-to-shortlist down · accept-rate up · recruiters coming back
