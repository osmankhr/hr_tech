# Senior ML Engineer — NLP / Large Language Models

## About the Role

We are looking for a Senior Machine Learning Engineer with deep expertise in
Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Large Language Models (LLMs). You will
design and deploy production-grade NLP systems and help shape our AI strategy.

## Requirements

- 7+ years of experience in machine learning and data science
- Strong NLP background: text classification, NER, information extraction,
  transformers, semantic search
- Hands-on experience with large language models (GPT, BERT, LLaMA, etc.)
- Production experience: model serving, MLOps, monitoring
- MSc or PhD in Computer Science, Statistics, or related field preferred
- Proficiency in Python; PyTorch or TensorFlow

## Nice to Have

- Experience with RAG systems, agentic AI, prompt engineering
- Publications in NLP/ML venues
- Experience with low-resource languages or multilingual models

## Location

Turkey (Istanbul preferred) or Turkish professionals abroad open to relocation.
