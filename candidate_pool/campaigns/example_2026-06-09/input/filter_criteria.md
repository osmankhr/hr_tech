# Candidate Filtering Criteria

## Accept (ALL must be met)

1. **Seniority**: 7+ years of experience, OR Senior / Staff / Principal / Lead title
2. **NLP Depth**: Must have meaningful NLP experience — at least one of:
   - Text classification, NER, information extraction
   - Transformer models (BERT, T5, GPT variants, LLaMA)
   - LLMs, RAG, prompt engineering, agentic AI
   - Traditional NLP: text mining, sentiment analysis, POS tagging, machine translation
3. **ML Foundation**: Strong background in ML (statistics, deep learning, research)
4. **Turkey Connection**: Currently based in Turkey, OR has a Turkish background
   (Turkish name, or degree from a Turkish university such as Boğaziçi, ODTÜ, Bilkent,
   İTÜ, Sabancı, Koç, Hacettepe, Yıldız, Istanbul University, IZTECH)

## Reject (ANY one triggers rejection)

- Junior / intern / entry-level title with fewer than 5 years of experience
- No NLP experience (pure computer vision, tabular ML, or non-technical roles)
- Pure manager or executive with no demonstrated technical depth
- No Turkey connection whatsoever
- Currently employed at the hiring company

## PENDING (use when evidence is unclear)

- Profile text is too short to evaluate seniority or NLP depth
- Background is ambiguous (e.g., "text analytics" without confirming NLP methods)
- Turkish connection is possible but unconfirmed

## Scoring Guidance

- Prioritize candidates who combine LLM/GenAI experience with traditional NLP breadth
- Bonus signals: PhD, peer-reviewed publications, top-tier employers
  (Google, Meta, Anthropic, DeepMind, Microsoft Research, Amazon, Hugging Face)
- Strong Turkish universities for ML: Boğaziçi, ODTÜ, Bilkent, İTÜ, Sabancı, Koç
